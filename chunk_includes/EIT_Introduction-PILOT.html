DIRECTIONS: Elicited Imitation Task

In this experiment, you will hear sentences. After each sentence, you will be asked to wait for approxmately 3 seconds. After the period of wating, you will need to repeat the sentence you heard earlier. This is an "imitation" task, meaning that after the delay you need to imitate or repeat the sentence you heard. Listen carefully to each sentence and do not speak until the screen says that you may record your sentence. Speaking before the cue is given will lead to the data not being properly recorded.
